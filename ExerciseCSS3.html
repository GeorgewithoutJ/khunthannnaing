Climate Change

Understanding Its Impact on Our Planet

Naing

Climate change refers to long-term shifts and alterations in temperature and weather patterns. In the past century, human activities have accelerated this process due to increased emissions of greenhouse gases.

The Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) has documented how rising temperatures are leading to sea-level rise and extreme weather conditions. This has serious consequences for both the environment and human societies.

Scientists emphasize the role of carbon dioxide and other pollutants in intensifying the greenhouse effect. Without global action, the consequences could be catastrophic.

Governments around the world are working on climate policies to limit emissions and promote sustainability. Organizations like the United Nations continue to push for stronger commitments.
